Dogs

Dogs have been around a long time.

Dogs have been gracing us with their fluffy presence for at least 15,000 years to possibly over 30,000 years. The earliest, most undisputed evidence of a domesticated dog is the Bonn-Oberkassel dog, dating to approximately 14,200 years ago. The dog's skull and jawbone were found buried alongside two humans (a man and a woman), suggesting a deep emotional bond and care for the animal, which was likely ill.
